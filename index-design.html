What is GoEast.ai?
GoEast.ai is an AI-powered platform that makes Chinese philosophy accessible through interactive storytelling, AI oracle consultations, and practical skills for living in China.
Who is Sophie?
Sophie is the protagonist of our narrative journey — a young reader who travels through 3,000 years of Chinese thought, meeting philosophers and encountering their ideas firsthand.
How does the AI Oracle work?
Our AI Oracles are modeled after ancient Chinese thinkers. Ask a question and receive guidance inspired by Confucian, Daoist, Legalist, or other philosophical traditions.
Is GoEast.ai free?
The core experience — Sophie's Journey, the Glossary, and Insights — is free. AI Oracle consultations require a subscription.
