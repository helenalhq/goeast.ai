## Description

Comprehensive guide to China's visa types, entry requirements, and immigration procedures. Covers tourist visas (L), business visas (M), student visas (X1/X2), work visas (Z), and the popular 144-hour transit visa exemptions available at major cities including Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou. Includes document checklists, application timelines, consulate procedures, and the latest policy updates for foreign visitors. Essential pre-arrival planning resource.
