## Description

Write Chinese that sounds human — not formal, not robotic, not AI-generated. This skill teaches AI agents to produce natural, casual Chinese with proper sentence-final particles (啊、呢、吧、嘛), internet slang (666, 绝了, yyds), regional awareness (Mainland simplified vs Taiwan traditional), and native punctuation. Perfect for foreigners who need to communicate naturally in Chinese contexts — from WeChat messages to Xiaohongshu posts to business emails with the right tone.

## Description (中文)

写出自然、口语化的中文，避免 AI 味和书面语。掌握语气助词（啊、呢、吧、嘛）、网络用语（666、绝了、yyds）、简繁体区分和标点规范。适合需要与中国人自然交流的外国人——无论是微信聊天、小红书发文，还是商务邮件，都能找到合适的语气。
