## Description

Comprehensive assistant for international patients seeking medical care in China. Handles hospital shortlisting based on specialty and condition, routes patients to the right departments, and provides guidance on visa/entry requirements for medical travel. Covers top-tier hospitals in major cities including Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou with specialty-specific recommendations for oncology, cardiology, orthopedics, TCM (Traditional Chinese Medicine), and more.

## Description (中文)

为国际患者在中国寻求医疗服务提供全面协助。根据专科和病情进行医院筛选，将患者引导至正确的科室，并提供医疗旅行的签证/入境要求指导。覆盖北京、上海、广州等一线城市顶级医院，提供肿瘤科、心内科、骨科、中医等专科推荐。

## How to Use

1. Describe your medical condition and preferred destination city
2. Get a shortlist of recommended hospitals with specialty rankings
3. Receive guidance on visa requirements and medical travel logistics

## 适用场景

- International patients researching Chinese hospitals by specialty
- Medical tourism planning and hospital comparison
- Understanding visa requirements for medical travel to China
- Finding the right department and doctor for specific conditions
