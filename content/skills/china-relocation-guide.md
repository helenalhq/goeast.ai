## Description

Complete relocation guide for expats, international students, and digital nomads moving to China. Covers city selection criteria (cost of living, internet access, international communities, pollution levels), visa types and application processes, housing search strategies (short-term and long-term), neighborhood recommendations with foreigner-friendly amenities, and essential setup tasks (bank accounts, SIM cards, transportation cards).
