## Description

AI-powered travel agent that helps you find, book, and change flights via email. Searches across airlines and booking platforms to find the best options for your travel dates and preferences. Handles flight changes, cancellations, and rebooking through automated email interactions. Supports international and domestic flights including routes to and within China.

## Description (中文)

AI 驱动的旅行代理，通过邮件帮助你查找、预订和更改航班。搜索航空公司和预订平台，为你的出行日期和偏好找到最佳选择。通过自动化邮件交互处理航班变更、取消和重新预订。支持国际和国内航班，包括往返中国及中国国内的航线。

## Install

```bash
clawhub install clawhub/travel-agent
```

## How to Use

1. Describe your travel needs — origin, destination, dates, preferences
2. The agent searches flights and presents options via email
3. Confirm your choice and receive booking confirmation

## 适用场景

- Finding the best flight deals to China
- Changing or rebooking existing flights
- Managing business travel arrangements
- Comparing multiple airline options efficiently
