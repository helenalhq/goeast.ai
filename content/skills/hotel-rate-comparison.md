## Description

Real-time hotel price comparison across major Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) including Trip.com (Ctrip), Booking.com, Agoda, and more. Essential for finding the best deals on hotels in China, where prices can vary significantly between platforms. Compare rates for the same property across multiple booking sites simultaneously, view total costs including taxes and fees, and identify the cheapest option. Supports both Chinese domestic and international hotel bookings.

## Description (中文)

跨主流在线旅游平台（OTA）实时酒店比价，包括携程、Booking.com、Agoda 等。在中国预订酒店时，不同平台价格差异可能很大，这个工具能帮你同时比较同一酒店在多个预订网站上的价格，查看含税总价，找到最优惠的选择。支持中国国内和国际酒店预订。

## Install

```bash
npx skillfish add hotel-rate
```

## How to Use

1. Enter your destination city and travel dates
2. Get price comparisons across multiple booking platforms
3. Click through to the best deal and complete your booking

## 适用场景

- 比较携程、Booking、Agoda 等平台的中国酒店价格
- 找到同一酒店在不同平台上的最低价格
- 预订中国出差或旅行的性价比酒店
- 了解中国酒店的真实含税总价
- 在旅游旺季找到最优价格
