## Description

Complete guide for foreigners setting up mobile payments in China. China is essentially a cashless society — from street food to high-speed rail tickets, nearly everything runs on WeChat Pay and Alipay. This skill walks you through linking international credit cards (Visa, Mastercard) to both platforms, explains transaction limits for foreign users, covers common payment scenarios (QR code scanning, in-app payments, peer transfer), and troubleshoots frequent issues like declined cards and verification failures.
