## Description

Bilingual (Chinese/English) inbound travel requirements collector with Feishu (Lark) integration. This skill helps travel agencies and service providers collect and organize traveler requirements efficiently. Supports structured data collection for visa applications, accommodation preferences, transportation needs, and itinerary planning. Outputs are integrated with Feishu for team collaboration.

## How to Use

1. Configure your Feishu integration (set `FEISHU_BASE_TOKEN` and `FEISHU_TABLE_ID`)
2. Start collecting traveler requirements in Chinese or English
3. Data is automatically organized and shared with your team

## 适用场景

- Travel agencies serving inbound tourists to China
- Collecting structured visa and travel requirements
- Team-based travel service coordination via Feishu
- Bilingual customer service for international travelers
