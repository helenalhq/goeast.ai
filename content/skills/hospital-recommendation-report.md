## Description

Generates premium hospital recommendation reports for patients considering medical treatment in China. Each report covers 3 recommended hospitals with detailed comparison including specialty strengths, estimated costs, treatment timelines, logistics (travel, accommodation near hospital), and English-speaking support availability. Reports can be exported as PDF for sharing with family and advisors.

## Description (中文)

为考虑在中国接受治疗的患者生成高端医院推荐报告。每份报告覆盖 3 家推荐医院，包含详细比较：专科优势、预估费用、治疗时间线、后勤安排（交通、医院附近住宿）以及英语服务支持情况。报告可导出为 PDF 格式，方便与家人和顾问分享。

## How to Use

1. Install the skill from GitHub
2. Provide your medical condition, budget range, and preferred city
3. Receive a structured report comparing 3 recommended hospitals
4. Export the report as PDF for offline reference

## 适用场景

- Comparing hospitals for specific treatments before traveling
- Getting cost estimates and logistics planning for medical travel
- Sharing hospital research with family members or medical advisors
- Making informed decisions about where to seek treatment in China
