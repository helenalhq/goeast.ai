## Description

Specialized knowledge for the Alipay Open Platform — China's dominant mobile payment system with over 1 billion users. Navigate the SPA-based Chinese documentation to integrate payment capabilities, understand merchant setup flows, configure payment methods for foreign visitors, and troubleshoot common integration issues. Essential for businesses wanting to accept payments from Chinese customers or foreigners building services that interface with China's payment ecosystem.
