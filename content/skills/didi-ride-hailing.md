## Description

Official DiDi ride-hailing AI skill for navigating China's transportation system. Access core functions including Chinese address search, real-time price estimation, ride booking, and route planning. DiDi is China's equivalent of Uber and the primary ride-hailing platform serving over 550 million users. This skill helps foreigners overcome the language barrier when using DiDi, with bilingual address support and clear step-by-step booking guidance.

## Description (中文)

滴滴出行官方 AI 技能，帮助你使用中国最大的出行平台。支持中文地址搜索、实时价格估算、叫车预订和路线规划。滴滴是中国的网约车平台，服务超过 5.5 亿用户。这个技能帮助外国人克服使用滴滴时的语言障碍，提供双语地址支持和清晰的叫车指导。

## How to Use

1. Enter your destination in Chinese or English
2. Get price estimates and available ride types
3. Book your ride and follow navigation to pickup point

## 适用场景

- 在中国使用滴滴叫车出行
- 查询目的地地址和预估费用
- 了解不同车型（快车、专车、拼车）的价格差异
- 克服语言障碍完成叫车和导航
- 在不熟悉的城市使用网约车代替公共交通
